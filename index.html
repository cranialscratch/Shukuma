Goal

Find the longest word you can using the letters on the board. Letters must be connected — each letter must touch the next.

How to play

  • Tap or drag across letters to build a word.
  • You can only use each letter once.
  • Letters must be adjacent (touching).
  • Tap a selected letter to deselect it and everything after.
  • A tile showing ★ is a blank — it counts as any letter.

Tile colours

  • Purple — word in progress
  • Green — valid word found!
  • Red — not a valid word

Score levels

  • ≤ 3 letters — Below Average
  • 4–5 letters — Average
  • 6–7 letters — Above Average
  • 8–9 letters — Master
  • ≥ 10 letters — Grandmaster
  • Find the longest word first try — Grandmaster in One!

Sharing

Tap Share to post your score. First share each day earns a Ticket. Your word is never revealed!
