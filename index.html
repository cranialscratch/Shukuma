Goal

Find the longest word you can using the letters on the board. Letters must be connected — each letter must touch the next.

How to play

  • Tap or drag across letters to build a word.
  • You can only use each letter once.
  • Letters must be adjacent (touching).
  • Tap a selected letter to deselect it and everything after.
  • A tile showing ★ is a blank — it counts as any letter.

Tile colours

  • Purple — word in progress
  • Green — valid word found!
  • Red — not a valid word

Score levels

  • ≤ 3 letters — Below Average
  • 4–5 letters — Average
  • 6–7 letters — Above Average
  • 8–9 letters — Master
  • ≥ 10 letters — Grandmaster
  • Find the longest word first try — Grandmaster in One!

Sharing

Tap Share to post your score. First share each day earns a Ticket. Your word is never revealed!

Hints

Tap the lightbulb to open the hint menu. Three types are available:

  • Letter — 1 Ticket: Highlights the next unrevealed letter of today's longest word on the board for a few seconds. Use it again to reveal the next letter. You can spend up to 10 tickets on letter hints per game. After that, letters are shown for free — but your rank changes to Defeated.
  • Define — 5 Tickets: Shows the dictionary definition of today's longest word. Only charged the first time — tap again as often as you like for free.
  • Defeat — 10 Tickets: Reveals the word itself with a dramatic animation. You can still find it and explore other words, but your rank will show as Defeated.
