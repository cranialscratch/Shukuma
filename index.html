How to play

Goal

Find the longest word you can using the letters on the board. Letters must be connected — each letter must touch the next.

How to play

  • Tap or drag across letters to build a word.
  • You can only use each letter once.
  • Letters must be adjacent (touching).
  • Tap a selected letter to deselect it and everything after.
  • A blank tile counts as any letter.

Tile colours

  • Yellow — word in progress
  • Green — valid word found!
  • Red — not a valid word

Score levels

  • ≤ 4 letters — Below Average
  • 5–6 letters — Average
  • 7–10 letters — Above Average
  • 11–14 letters — Master
  • ≥ 15 letters — Grandmaster

Sharing

Tap Share to post your score and earn a Joyall Ticket. Your word is never revealed!
